🛍️ E-Commerce Data Analysis Project 

📌 Project Overview 

This project analyzes a Brazilian e-commerce transactional dataset to uncover insights into: 

Revenue performance 

Customer behavior 

Delivery performance 

Payment patterns 

Customer segmentation using RFM analysis 

The goal is to generate business-driven insights that can support strategic decision-making. 



 

📊 Key Analyses Performed 

1️⃣ Revenue Analysis 

Revenue by State 

Revenue by Product Category 

Revenue by Payment Type 

Revenue Over Time (Monthly Trends) 

Order-Level Revenue 



2️⃣ Delivery Performance 

Delivery Time 

Delivery Delay 

Late Delivery Indicator (is_late) 



3️⃣ Correlation Analysis 

Explored relationships between: 

Order Value 

Installments 

Freight Cost 

Delivery Metrics 

Customer Purchase Behavior 



4️⃣ RFM Customer Segmentation 

Customers were segmented based on: 

Recency – Days since last purchase 

Frequency – Number of purchases 

Monetary – Total spending 

Customers were grouped into: 

Low Spenders 

Mid Spenders 

High Spenders 

VIP Customers 

 



🔍 Key Findings 

💰 Revenue Insights 

Revenue varies significantly by state and product category. 

A small number of customers contribute a large share of total revenue. 

Installment payments are moderately correlated with higher order values. 

Revenue shows time-based patterns suggesting seasonality. 

 



👥 Customer Behavior Insights 

Most customers purchase only once. 

Strong correlation (0.82) between total orders and repeat customers. 

VIP and high-spender segments generate disproportionate revenue. 

Monetary distribution is highly right-skewed. 

This reflects the Pareto principle (80/20 rule) common in e-commerce. 

 



🚚 Operational Insights 

Late deliveries are relatively low. 

Strong correlation between delivery time and delivery delay confirms metric reliability. 

No strong evidence that delivery delays significantly reduce revenue. 



 

📈 Business Recommendations 

Focus on customer retention strategies. 

Develop loyalty programs targeting high-value customers. 

Encourage repeat purchases among one-time buyers. 

Continue offering installment payment options. 

Optimize delivery performance in high-delay regions.

🎯 Conclusion 

This analysis highlights that revenue concentration among repeat and high-value customers drives business stability. Strategic focus should shift toward retention and lifetime value maximization rather than solely customer acquisition.
